About our product

In my country there is a problem with small buisnes, because of problems with managment. Company owners doesnt want to spending money for managers, justifying this by the fact that their company doesnt so big, and all employers can manage their job on their own. And as a rule, thats strategy leads to the chaos, and total misunderstanding between owners and employees.

Our goal is to have all work live in Provis, while simplify the application as much as possible, with retaining all the functionality - thereby making people more productive and giving back at least 15% of time to dedicate to other things.

With our system you can comfortable: delegating, commenting tasks, follow up on assignments, keep track of deadlines, observe the efficiency of employees, better understand how much time a specific task takes, and based on this knowledge better plan your business in the future. And this is only part of the possibilities that Provis can propose you.
