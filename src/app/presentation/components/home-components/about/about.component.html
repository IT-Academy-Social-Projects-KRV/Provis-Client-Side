About our product

In my country there is a problem with small business, because of problems with management. Company owners don't want to spend money for managers, justifying this by the fact that their company isn't so big, and all employers can manage their job on their own. And as a rule, that strategy leads to chaos, and total misunderstanding between owners and employees.

Our goal is to have all work live in Provis, while simplifying the application as much as possible, with retaining all the functionality - thereby making people more productive and giving back at least 15% of time to dedicate to other things.

With our system you can comfortable: delegating, commenting on tasks, following up on assignments, keeping track of deadlines, observing the efficiency of employees, better understanding how much time a specific task takes, and based on this knowledge better plan your business in the future. And this is only part of the possibilities that Provis can propose to you.
