About our product

In my country there is a problem with small business, caused by problems with management. Company owners don't want to spend money for managers, justifying this by the fact that their company isn't so big, and all employers can manage their job on their own. And as a rule, that strategy leads to chaos, and total misunderstanding between owners and employees.

Our goal is to have all work live in Provis, while simplifying the application as much as possible, with retaining all the functionality - thereby making people more productive and giving back at least 15% of time to dedicate to other things.

With our system you can comfortably: delegate, comment tasks, follow up on assignments, keep track of deadlines. Also it allows you to observe the efficiency of employees, better understand how much time a specific task takes. Based on this knowledge you will plan your business better in the future. This is the least that Provis can propose you.
